SDG 8: Decent Work & Economic Growth

Addressing youth unemployment with practical, measurable solutions.

The Problem

Youth unemployment and underemployment remain critical barriers to sustainable economic growth. This demo shows a simple tool to estimate job creation from investment and collect community proposals for solutions like training, microfinance, and small-business support.

Quick Stats

Global youth unemployment ~13% (varies by region)
Informal employment ~60% in developing regions
Typical training cost USD 2,000–8,000 per job
